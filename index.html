Our Story

Grilled the slow way, since day one.

Best Barbique House was built around one idea: barbecue tastes better over real charcoal, cooked slow, and shared at a table with people you like. Every skewer, sausage and whole chicken that leaves our grill is fire-finished to order — nothing pre-cooked, nothing rushed.

We sit along the Bagmati Corridor in Jorpati, a short drive from Pashupatinath Temple, with space for dine-in, a drive-through for when you're in a hurry, and a handful of rooms upstairs for guests who want to stay the night.
