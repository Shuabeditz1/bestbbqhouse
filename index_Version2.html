The place

Coal-fired, close to the temple

Tucked into the Bagmati corridor in Jorpati, a short ride from Pashupatinath Temple, the grill here runs on live charcoal, not gas — the same way sekuwa has always been cooked in this part of Kathmandu.

Dine in on the patio, get it packed to go, or stay the night: private rooms sit just upstairs from the smoke.
